FitTrack for iPhone

FitTrack Support

Help and contact information for FitTrack, a workout, recovery, and wellness tracker for iPhone.

Getting Started

  • Create workout plans from the Workouts tab.
  • Browse exercises and open detail views for movement guidance.
  • Use Wellness tools to log hydration, weight, pain, posture, and recovery notes.
  • Connect Apple Health only if you want FitTrack to read daily metrics or write completed workouts.

Apple Health And Permissions

Apple Health, Calendar, notifications, and iCloud access are optional. You can review or revoke permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

iCloud Backup

If iCloud backup is enabled, FitTrack stores encrypted backup data in your private iCloud database. Make sure you are signed in to iCloud and have iCloud Drive enabled if backup or restore is unavailable.
